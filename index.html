Beginning

From scratch we each have our own ideas of what we would share to our own groupmates so then we can decide what we will animate. below is shown the storyboards of each of my groupmates.

My storyboard, my story was that leaving lights on when you don't necessarily need it wastes the electricity consumption and that it leads to climate change. On the first panel it shows a stickfigure leaving the lights on before he leaves, which is wasteful. The second panel then zooms out to see other rooms all over the world as you might say, and the lights are left on aswell. What I'm trying to say is that when you make a mistake you're not the only one who has done it or is currently. It is normal to happen, almost like when you decline a call; there is WAY more than just one person declining a call currently. On the third panel it presents earth; and that "everyone" (exaggeration) is wasting electricity... which then leads to the fourth panel presenting earth abnormally burning. Onto to the fifth panel

what do we have here? earth is getting steamed up because of the fossil fuels used to produce electricity! Then finally, the sixth panel wraps up my storyboard by just saying end, which then I thought that the earth can explode just to y'know teach people in a way they would remember.
